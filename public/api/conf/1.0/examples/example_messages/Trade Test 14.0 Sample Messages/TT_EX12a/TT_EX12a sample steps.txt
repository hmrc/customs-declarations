Sample Steps
*************

Step 0	- Verify CHIEF connectivity test – Submission of EAL message with DUCR that does NOT exist in CHIEF

Step 1	- Dec 1 in CHIEF with DUCR(1) with Numeric and Alpha DUCR Part ID 

Step 2	- Dec 2 in CDS – DUCR(2) 

Step 3	- Submit EAA(1) with CHIEF DUCR(1) with only with Numeric DUCR Part ID 

Step 4	- Submit EAA(2) with CDS DUCR(2) 

Step 5	- Submit EAL(1) with CDS DUCR(2) 

Step 6	- Submit EAL(2) with CHIEF DUCR(1) with only with Numeric DUCR Part ID

Step 7	- Submit EAC(1) (CDS) with DUCR (2) + MUCR(1)

Step 8	- Submit EAC(2) (CHIEF) with DUCR (1) with only with Numeric DUCR Part ID + MUCR(1).

Step 9	- Submit CST message Close MUCR(1).

Step 10	- Submit EAL(3) with MUCR(1).

Step 11	- EDL with MUCR(1).
