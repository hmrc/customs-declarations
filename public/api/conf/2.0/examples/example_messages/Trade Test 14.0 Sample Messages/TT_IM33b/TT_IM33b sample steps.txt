Sample steps
************

1. Declaration is submitted:
Indicative duty is calculated and DMSTAX and DMSACC Trader Notifications are sent prior to the Dwell Timer.

Dwell Timer starts.

2. Amendment submitted

Indicative customs debt recalculated based on the new item charge amount
DMSRCV, DMSREQ & DMSTAX Trader Notifications sent.

Dwell timer ends.

DMSTAX Trader Notification is sent.
Declaration clears DMSRES & DMSCLE Trader Notifications sent.
