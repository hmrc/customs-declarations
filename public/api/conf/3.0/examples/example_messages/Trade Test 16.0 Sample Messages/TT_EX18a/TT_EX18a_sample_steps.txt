Sample steps
************

Step 0: Verify CHIEF connectivity test – Submission of EAL message with DUCR that does NOT exist in CHIEF.

Step 1: Dec 1 in CHIEF with DUCR(1).

Step 2: Dec 2 in CDS – DUCR(2).

Step 3: Submit EAA 1 with CHIEF DUCR(1).

Step 4: Submit EAA 2 with CDS DUCR(2).

Step 5: Submit EAL 1 with CDS DUCR(2).

Step 6: Submit EAL 2 with CHIEF DUCR(1).

Step 7: Submit EAC 1 (CDS) with DUCR (2) + MUCR(1).

Step 8: Submit EAC 2 (CHIEF) with DUCR (1) + MUCR(1).

Step 9: Submit CST message Close MUCR(1).

Step 10: Submit EAL 3 with MUCR(1).

Step 11: Submit a cancellation for CDS declaration.

Step 12: EDL with MUCR(1).
